Volunteer at Aileron Association

I am a volunteer at the Aileron Association, an organization dedicated to the protection of rays and sharks in the Mediterranean. The association carries out scientific, educational, and citizen science projects to improve knowledge about these often misunderstood species and to combat their decline. Through various activities such as conferences, school outreach, sea excursions, and awareness stands, we aim to raise public awareness and promote effective conservation measures. This experience has deepened my commitment to animal protection, a cause I am passionate about, especially the conservation of marine species.
